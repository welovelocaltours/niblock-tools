How a lead becomes a proposal — from first enquiry to a sent PDF

  1. Lead arrives — a website enquiry emails info@niblocklogistics.co.uk.
  2. Discovery form — run it on the call → Generate; the figures carry into the calculator automatically.
  3. Quote Calculator — opens pre-filled; check the rate card and you get the price, monthly value & first-year value.
  4. Proposal Generator — open it, paste in the prospect details and the quote figures, and click Generate draft proposal. A branded proposal is drafted for you in seconds.
  5. Review & send — read it through, tweak any wording, Save as PDF and email it to the prospect.
