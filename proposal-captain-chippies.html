Understanding your brand

We get what makes Captain Chippies special

You turned a late-night craving and a shared love of spiced rum into one of South London's most talked-about hot sauces — hand-cooked in small batches (1kg of fresh Scotch Bonnets and a whole bottle of premium spiced rum, every time), sold out at pop-ups, and powered by your Beats & Eats community and a loyal "Crew" of subscribers. Limited drops like the Naga Ghost sell fast, and the wholesale and export enquiries are starting to land.

Right now, you make it, bottle it, pack it and ship it — every online order, every pop-up, every Crew box. That hands-on care is the brand. But it's also the ceiling: the wholesale stockists, the EU export plans, the next limited drop — all of it means more packing nights and less time making sauce (and on the decks). That's exactly where the right fulfilment partner turns the hustle into headroom.
