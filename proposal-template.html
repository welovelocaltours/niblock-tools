Reusable proposal template. Niblock brand is fixed; the prospect brand is just the two CSS variables at the top (--accent, --accent-soft) plus the logo box and the bracketed [merge fields]. Per prospect I pull their logo + brand colour from their website and write the “Understanding your brand / How you win” copy. To make a PDF: open in your browser → Ctrl/Cmd-P → “Save as PDF” (A4, margins: none, background graphics: on). The example below is a fictional prospect, “Wildfire Provisions”, to show the personalisation. The client quote on the final page is swapped to the testimonial that best matches the prospect's top priority (the discovery form recommends which one).
